ABOUT BOSTON

Boston is the capital and largest city of the state of Massachusetts, located in Suffolk County. It is one of the oldest cities in the United States and is known for its rich history, diverse neighborhoods, and significant cultural and educational institutions.

With an estimated population of more than 650,000 residents, Boston plays a central role in the economic and social development of the New England region. The city is also home to world-renowned universities, innovative industries, and a vibrant arts community.

Greater Boston has a population of more than 4.5 million, making it the sixth-largest combined statistical area in the nation. The region continues to grow as a hub for technology, healthcare, finance, and education.
